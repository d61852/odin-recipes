Sushi Platter

"Kappa Maki and Nigiri Sushi featuring Salmon and Salmon skin"

Ingredients:

  • Salmon
  • Cucumber
  • Salmon Skin
  • Wasabi
  • Rice
  • Sushi Vinegar
  • Seawead
  • Garnished with pickled ginger and salmon roe

Recipe:

  1. Prepare the salmon!
  2. Prep the fish and cucumbers!
  3. Fry the salmon skin!
  4. Grate some wasabi!
  5. Make sushi rice!
  6. Make the maki!
  7. Slice the roll!
  8. Form the nigiri!
  9. Plate it and Snap it!
