Galaxy Smoothie

"Out of this world fruits and swirls!"

Ingredients:

  • Raspberries
  • Blueberries
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Honey
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Activated Charcoal
  • Gold Dust
  • Candy Stars

Recipe:

  1. Rinse the berries!
  2. Make the smoothie base!
  3. Mix the charcoal yogurt!
  4. Mix the blue yogurt!
  5. Mix the pink yogurt!
  6. Swirl it up!
  7. Plate it and Snap it!
