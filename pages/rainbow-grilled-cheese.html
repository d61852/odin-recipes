Rainbow Grilled Cheese

"Rainbow grilled cheese with all the colors!"

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Pink Dye
  • Yellow Dye
  • Green Dye
  • Blue Dye
  • Purple Dye
  • Butter
  • Served with a pickle wedge and fries

Recipe:

  1. Slice the bread!
  2. Grate some cheese!
  3. Dye the shredded cheese!
  4. Arrange the rainbow cheese!
  5. Melt butter in a pan!
  6. Grill it until golden!
  7. Plate it and Snap it!
